Welcome to Public Channel.

An android application for UPES students in which you can check your attendance, timetable and search among thousands of students. Non existing students can register easily.You can search students with their fb profile picture, post anonymously, and also update your skill profile. Skill search is a feature of searching students on the basis of their skills or talent. You can link your account with Facebook.
